Performance & Sports Injury Prevention

Train smarter, move better, and stay in the game all season long

Whether you're a weekend warrior, a competitive high school athlete, or a seasoned professional, your body deserves more than just reactive care after an injury. At Powerhouse PT in Laredo, Texas, our sports performance and injury prevention program is built around a simple idea: the best injury is the one that never happens. We combine movement science, strength training, and physical therapy expertise to help athletes at every level perform at their peak — and keep performing.

Who This Is For

  • High school and collegiate athletes
  • Runners and endurance athletes
  • Overhead athletes (baseball, tennis, swimming)
  • Soccer, basketball, and football players
  • Weightlifters and CrossFit athletes
  • Combat sports and martial arts athletes
  • Athletes returning from injury
  • Athletes looking to move more efficiently
  • Parents wanting to protect young athletes
  • Active adults maintaining an athletic lifestyle
  • Pre-season preparation and in-season maintenance
  • Off-season performance development

What We Offer

Movement Screening

We perform a comprehensive movement assessment to identify compensations, muscle imbalances, and mobility limitations that silently increase your injury risk before they become a problem.

Sport-Specific Conditioning

Your training is built around the demands of your specific sport — power, speed, agility, endurance — so you develop the exact physical qualities that translate to better performance.

Injury Prevention Programming

We design evidence-based protocols to strengthen the joints, tissues, and movement patterns most vulnerable to your sport — dramatically reducing your risk of ACL tears, shoulder injuries, and stress fractures.

Strength & Power Development

Our team includes USA Weightlifting certified coaches. We integrate proper strength and power training principles to help you build a body that performs at a higher level — safely.

Return-to-Sport Clearance

Coming back from an injury? We guide you through a structured return-to-sport progression with objective benchmarks — so you go back fully prepared, not just pain-free.

Performance Education

We teach athletes how to train, recover, and take care of their bodies between sessions — because what you do outside of PT matters just as much as what happens inside.

What to Expect

01

Performance Consultation

We start by learning about your sport, your training history, your goals, and any previous injuries. This gives us the context to build something that actually fits your life.

02

Movement & Performance Assessment

A detailed evaluation of how you move — including sport-specific patterns — to identify your strengths, limitations, and highest-priority areas to develop.

03

Personalized Program Design

Based on your assessment, we design a performance program that addresses your specific needs — not a cookie-cutter protocol pulled from the internet.

04

Ongoing Coaching & Progression

We work alongside you as you progress, refining the program and raising the bar so you're always improving — both on and off the field.
