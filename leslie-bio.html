Dr. Leslie Garcia, PT, DPT

Doctor of Physical Therapy

Meet Our Team

A proud Laredo native, Dr. Leslie Garcia brings a lifelong passion for health, movement, and community care. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Biology from Texas A&M International University, where she also worked as a fitness instructor and personal trainer, gaining valuable hands-on experience while completing her degree.

Dr. Leslie's interest in movement began early—she practiced Aikido for eight years during high school and college, which shaped her deep understanding of body awareness and functional control.

She went on to earn her Doctorate in Physical Therapy from the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston, where she built a strong foundation in rehabilitation and movement science. Throughout her career, Dr. Leslie has worked with a diverse range of patients, from pediatric to geriatric populations, tailoring her approach to meet the unique needs of each individual.

Dr. Leslie is especially passionate about integrating her fitness background with evidence-based rehab, helping patients not only recover, but thrive long-term. She specializes in the treatment of both orthopedic and neurological conditions, offering comprehensive care that empowers her patients through movement and education.
