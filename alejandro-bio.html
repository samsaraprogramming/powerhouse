Alejandro Morales, PTA

Physical Therapist Assistant

Our newest licensed physical therapy provider joining POWERHOUSE PT. A movement-focused clinician with a passion for building strength, restoring function, and helping patients perform at a higher level.

Alejandro takes a results-driven approach to care, creating efficient, individualized treatment plans that prioritize real progress and long-term outcomes.

With hands-on experience in orthopedic and rehabilitation settings, Alejandro works to improve mobility, strength, coordination, and overall performance. Outside the clinic, he enjoys mountain biking, fishing, and off-roading.

His approach emphasizes proper movement mechanics and progression — helping patients not only recover, but come back stronger.

Alejandro is committed to continuous growth and plans to pursue his Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree, further elevating the level of care he provides.
