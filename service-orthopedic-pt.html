Orthopedic Physical Therapy

Targeted care for joint, muscle, and nerve pain — so you can move and feel your best again

Schedule a Free Call
Back to Services

At Powerhouse PT, our orthopedic physical therapy program goes beyond just treating symptoms. We dig deep to find the root cause of your pain — whether it's a nagging back injury, a stiff knee, or a shoulder that just won't cooperate — and build a plan that gets you moving confidently again. Based in Laredo, Texas, we work with patients of all ages and activity levels who are ready to stop managing their pain and start living without it.

Conditions We Treat

  • Low back pain and sciatica
  • Neck pain and cervical dysfunction
  • Knee pain, ACL and meniscus injuries
  • Shoulder impingement and rotator cuff tears
  • Hip pain and labral pathology
  • Ankle sprains and instability
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Tendinitis and overuse injuries
  • Nerve pain and radiculopathy
  • Muscle strains and ligament sprains
  • Arthritis and joint stiffness
  • Plantar fasciitis and foot pain

How We Help

Thorough Evaluation

Every treatment plan starts with a comprehensive assessment of your movement, strength, flexibility, and pain patterns — so we understand exactly what's driving your condition.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, soft tissue work, and myofascial release to restore mobility, reduce pain, and accelerate your recovery.

Individualized Exercise Programs

Targeted strengthening and corrective exercises designed around your specific deficits — not a generic protocol. We build you up progressively so results stick long-term.

Movement Re-Education

We identify faulty movement patterns that contribute to your pain and teach you how to move safely and efficiently — reducing your risk of re-injury down the road.

Progress Tracking

We continuously reassess and adapt your treatment so you're always moving forward — not spinning your wheels with a plan that stopped working weeks ago.

Patient Education

Understanding your condition is a powerful part of recovery. We take the time to explain what's happening in your body and what you can do to support your own healing.
