Dry Needling


Dry needling targets tight muscle bands and trigger points to release tension, reduce pain, and restore normal muscle function.

Using thin monofilament needles, we stimulate deep tissue to accelerate healing by triggering a release of endorphins to promote recovery.

This technique is especially effective for chronic pain, muscle tightness, and sports-related injuries.

Cupping


Cupping therapy uses specialized cups to create suction on the skin, promoting blood flow, reducing muscle tension, and accelerating the body's natural healing process.

This technique effectively relieves deep muscle soreness, breaks up scar tissue, and decreases inflammation — making it ideal for both injury recovery and performance enhancement.
