Georgina Morales, PTA

Physical Therapist Assistant

Georgina brings a strong athletic background, advanced training, and a genuine passion for helping patients move and feel their best.

Georgina has been an athlete her entire life. She has played volleyball for 17 years—and what truly stands out is that she still plays actively and competitively today, bringing the same energy and discipline into her work. She also trains in weight lifting and running, staying committed to her own health and athletic performance.

Clinically, Georgina has years of experience treating both orthopedic and neurological conditions. She helps patients improve strength, mobility, coordination, balance, and overall function through a movement-based, individualized approach shaped by her background in volleyball, basketball, track, shot put, and discus.

Georgina values continuing education and plans to pursue her Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree in the near future, continuing her growth as a provider for the Laredo community.
